Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

This page explains what information Green and Snow Studio collects through the website greenandsnowstudio.com.

What we collect

This website does not collect any personal data automatically. We do not use cookies, analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party scripts.

If you email us at info@greenandsnowstudio.com, we receive whatever you choose to send — your email address, your name if you include it, and the content of your message.

How we use it

We use what you send only to respond to you. We do not share it, sell it, or use it for marketing.

How long we keep it

Emails are retained as long as the conversation is useful to either party. If you would like your messages deleted, write to us and we will remove them.

Service providers

Email is hosted by Google Workspace, which has its own privacy practices.

Children

This website is intended for a general business audience, not for children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

Contact

Questions about this policy: info@greenandsnowstudio.com.
