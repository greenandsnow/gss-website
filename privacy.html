Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

Plain English, no surprises. This policy explains what information we collect when you use Green and Snow Studio services or visit our website, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to provide our service and run our business. Specifically:

From you, directly

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number, company or brand name, when you submit a form or email us.
  • Project information: details about the apps, websites, or ideas you discuss with us, when you inquire about our services or begin a project.
  • Payment information: billing address and payment method details, processed by our payment processor. We do not store full credit card numbers.

Automatically, from your device

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system.
  • Usage data: pages you visit, time spent, referring website, basic analytics signals.

How we use it

We use the information we collect for these purposes:

  • To provide our service. Respond to your inquiries, design and build your software, deliver projects, send invoices.
  • To communicate with you. Reply to questions, share project updates, notify you of issues or changes.
  • To improve our operations. Understand how the website is used, identify what works and what doesn't, fix problems.
  • To comply with law. Tax filings, fraud prevention, responding to legal requests.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your information for advertising. We do not share your details with competitors or third parties for marketing purposes.

How we share it

We share your information only with parties who help us run our service:

  • Cloud and software vendors — email hosting, code hosting, deployment platforms, accounting and communication tools used to run our business.
  • Payment processors — to bill you and process payments.
  • Legal authorities — when required by law, court order, or to protect against fraud.

If we ever sell or transfer the business, your information may be part of that transfer — but only under the same protections described here.

Cookies and tracking

Our website uses cookies for two purposes: essential functionality (so the site and forms work) and basic analytics (to understand how the site is used).

We don't use advertising cookies, third-party trackers for marketing, or behavior-based remarketing pixels.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings — the site will still work, but some features may not.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as we need it:

  • Active clients: for the duration of our service relationship plus 7 years after, for tax and legal purposes.
  • Inquiries that don't become clients: 12 months from your last contact, then deleted.
  • Website analytics: aggregated and anonymized after 14 months.

If you want your data deleted earlier, contact us — we'll do it within 30 days, except where law requires us to retain it.

Your rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have rights under privacy laws (GDPR if in the EU, CCPA/CPRA if in California, PIPEDA if in Canada, and similar laws elsewhere). These include:

  • Access: a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to fix inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete your data (with exceptions for legal records).
  • Portability: a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Objection: opt out of certain types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent: for any processing based on consent.

To exercise any of these, email info@greenandsnowstudio.com. We respond within 30 days.

Children's privacy

Our service is for businesses, not for individuals under 18. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we've done so accidentally, we delete it promptly.

Security

We use standard industry practices to protect your data: encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls on who can see what, encrypted password storage, and limited data sharing with vendors who maintain their own safeguards.

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever detect a breach affecting your data, we'll notify you within 72 hours and tell you what happened and what we're doing about it.

International transfers

Green and Snow Studio operates from the United States and Canada. Some of our team members and service providers work from other countries. Your data may be stored or processed outside your country.

Where applicable, we rely on standard contractual clauses and equivalent protections to ensure your data stays protected wherever it's processed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our service evolves or as laws change. We'll post the updated version here with a new effective date.

For significant changes (new categories of data collected, new ways we use data), we'll notify active clients by email at least 30 days before the change takes effect.
